These are the files I used to create a ~57k word dictionary for myself. It is comprised of levels 1 through 6 without the large level 6c or the capitalized words, and some of my "personal" vocabulary list.

There are no bigram for next word prediction in this dictionary. I feel that the keyboard will learn my next words quick enough and having Gemini add them was turning out to be a bunch of words I didn't want and didn't make sense to me.

No doubt it is missing words, but considering that the top 1,000 words account for 89% of English communication how many extra words do we need for that remaining 11% 😂 I'll just have to add some words from time to time. 

I personally feel this is the maximum size I want to use with glide typing, actually about 15k is more like it but it is what it is.
